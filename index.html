Save Marine Life!
People are causing harm to marine animals and we need to fix that!

Harm we are causing

Over 1 million marine animals are killed because of plastic each year and there are over 75 million tons of plastic around the world. Pesticides and nutrients that are used for agriculture are washing up into waters which results in marine plants and shellfish to die. Sewage discharge also ends up into the ocean and pollutes it. Ocean pollution not only harms the animals but can harm us too. At one point they can close down the beaches just because ocean water becomes unsafe for us. Overfishing can also cause the oceans to die.
